Agri Eco-Tourism and Agricultural Showcase

Theme: Empowering Local Farmers, Enricing Communities

Date: December 6 & 7, 2024

Venue: KTown, Festive Parade, Megaworld, Iloilo City

A Marked Impact in the Agricultural Sector

The Agricultural Advancement Alliance Incorporated has made significant strides in the agricultural sector, advocating for the rights and welfare of farmers while promoting sustainable practices. By organizing these events, the AAA continues to make a marked impact, fostering innovation and collaboration in agriculture. The organization believes that by harnessing the power of community and innovation, the agricultural sector can thrive and contribute to the overall development of the Philippines.

Agri Eco-Tourism and Agricultural Gala Night

Theme: Empowering Local Farmers, Enricing Communities

Date: December 7, 2024

Venue: Zuri Hotel, Iloilo City

Join Us for a Night of Celebration and Support

The Gala Night promises to be an evening filled with inspiration, networking, and celebration. Attendees can expect a lively atmosphere, featuring local delicacies, agricultural exhibits, and opportunities to engage with farmers and industry leaders. The AAA invites everyone—businesses, individuals, and organizations—to join in this noble cause and support local farmers by attending the event or becoming a sponsor.

A Night for Local Farmers

The Gala Night is not just an event; it is a celebration of the hard work and dedication of local farmers who play a crucial role in the nation’s economy. By bringing together various stakeholders in the agricultural sector, the AAA aims to create a platform where farmers can showcase their products, share their stories, and connect with potential buyers and investors. This initiative is particularly important as it seeks to uplift the lives of farmers and promote sustainable agricultural practices in the Philippines.

Boosting Agricultural Tourism

In addition to supporting local farmers, the Gala Night is designed to boost agricultural tourism in the Philippines. With its diverse agricultural landscape and rich cultural heritage, the country has much to offer in terms of agritourism. The event will highlight the unique agricultural products of the region, encouraging both locals and tourists to appreciate and invest in Philippine agriculture. By promoting agricultural tourism, the AAA hopes to create new opportunities for farmers and contribute to the overall growth of the sector.

Collaboration with Sponsors

The success of the Gala Night relies heavily on the support of various sponsors. AdChoice Media, the event’s planner, and PreenXpress, the execution partner, are working diligently to ensure that the event is a resounding success. The AAA is inviting brands and businesses to come forward as sponsors, providing them with an opportunity to showcase their commitment to supporting local agriculture. Sponsorship will not only help fund the event but will also allow brands to connect with a community of farmers, stakeholders, and consumers passionate about agricultural advancement.
